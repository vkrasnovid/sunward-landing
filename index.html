Our Story

Founded in 1999 by He Qinghua, SUNWARD Equipment Co., Ltd. emerged from a simple vision: to create world-class industrial equipment that combines reliability, innovation, and precision. What began as a small manufacturing operation has evolved into a global leader in drilling, mining, construction, and loading equipment.

Today, operating from our modern industrial complex in Changsha, China, we continue to set industry standards. With over 25 years of expertise and a commitment to engineering excellence, SUNWARD Equipment has delivered thousands of solutions to customers across 50+ countries, powering some of the world's most critical operations.
